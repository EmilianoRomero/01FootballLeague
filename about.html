About
Mission

To support young atheletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer with oppportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportmanship.

Vision

The Northside Young Soccer League aspires to develop a strong, well-rounded and mindful athletes through the builduing of character, self-discipline and leadership.

General Information

The Northside Young Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL you must be between ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
